FAZE CLAN

FaZe Clan won 30 championship

A team founded by 3 Call of Duty players 11 years ago, FaZe Clan, won their 30th championship today to leave another mark on the esports scene.

Those three players were Eric “CLipZ” Rivera, Jeff “House Cat” Emann and Ben “Resistance” Christensen.

Rather than being a team that initially won tournaments, they had grown into one of YouTube’s most popular video game channels, with videos focused on trick shooting. One of their most popular series was ILLCAMS.

In 2012, they reached one million subscribers on YouTube and began to appear in esports. They first appeared in Call of Duty and Counter-Strike: Global Offensive tournaments.

FaZe Clan won their first championship at Call of Duty’s UMG Nashville 2014 tournament, their 30th championship was also in CoD by winning Call of Duty League – 2021 Season, the final of which was played on August 22, 2021.

With this championship, FaZe Clan managed something most of the esports teams couldn’t done, 30 championships in 5 different games.

Call of Duty: Ghosts

One of FaZe Clan’s first major successes was the Call of Duty: Ghosts’s UMG Nashville 2014 tournament held in October 2010.

They eliminated Denial Esports in the final to reach the $15,000 prize.

Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare

In Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare, FaZe Clan had a very successful period. They became champions by winning against their rivals OpTic Gaming in four important tournaments.

They were the nightmare of their rivals OpTic Gaming in MLG Columbus Open 2014, UMG Dallas 2015, Gfinity Summer Championship 2015 and MLG Pro League 2015 Season 3 tournaments and defeated them in the final.

Their total earnings from these four tournaments was $120,000.

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

The team achieved their first major success in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, defeating their rivals Astralis 2-0 in the StarLadder i-League StarSeries Season 3 final.

In the final of ESL One: New York 2017, they defeated Team Liquid 3-0 easily.

In the ELEAGUE CS:GO Premier 2017, they again defeated Astralis 2-0 in the grand final and won the championship.

They defeated mousesports in the Esports Championship Series Season 4 final and NAVI in the EPICENTER 2018 final. The team has lots of S-Tier titles and is considered one of the most successful teams in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive history.

PUBG: Battlegrounds

FaZe Clan managed to be one of the teams that was remembered in PUBG: Battlegrounds tournaments.

Championships such as StarSeries & i-League PUBG Season 1, DreamHack PUBG Showdown Austin 2018, GLL Grand Slam: PUBG Classic, PUBG Continental Series 2: Europe are among the best examples of this success.

Fortnite

FaZe Clan were among the teams that won the Fortnite Championship Series with Mongraal in C2 S4 and K1nG in C2 S6.

Call of Duty: World War II

FaZe Clan, which are at the forefront with their success in the Call of Duty franchise, defeated OpTic Gaming in the CWL Pro League 2018 Stage 1 final in Call of Duty: World War II and reached the $200,000 championship prize.

Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War

The team is one of the strongest teams in Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War. FaZe Clan is the champion of Call of Duty League 2021 – Stage 1 Major, Call of Duty League 2021 – Stage 3 Major and Call of Duty League 2021 – Stage 4 Major.

Most recently, they won the $1,200,000 grand prize Call of Duty League Championship 2021 by defeating TOR Ultra in the finals and reaching their thirtieth championship as a team.




Since their foundation, FaZe Clan has had many successes in Call of Duty, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, PUBG: Battlegrounds and Fortnite. The future achievements of the team, which is followed fondly by esports fans, will be eagerly awaited.
